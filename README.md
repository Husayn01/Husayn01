## 💡 Projects

Here are some of my key projects. Check out the repositories for detailed explanations and source code!

### 🌍 [Digital Nigeria Infrastructure Mapping](https://github.com/hussainiahmed/infrastructure-mapping)
- **Description**: Interactive digital map of Niger State's critical infrastructure (healthcare and education).
- **Tech Stack**: ArcGIS, Python, Power BI, Google Maps API.

### 📊 [Network Analysis of Transport Structures in Kaduna](https://github.com/hussainiahmed/network-analysis-kaduna)
- **Description**: Analyzing the distribution of transport hubs and calculating travel times across Kaduna Metropolis.
- **Tech Stack**: ArcGIS, QGIS, Python.

### 🗺️ [Geologic Mapping with ArcGIS](https://github.com/hussainiahmed/geologic-mapping)
- **Description**: A comprehensive geologic mapping project that includes topography, fault analysis, and geochemical data.
- **Tech Stack**: ArcGIS, Excel.

### 🔥 [Assessing Heat Risk on Education](https://github.com/hussainiahmed/heat-risk-analysis)
- **Description**: Analysis of heat risk and its impact on educational institutions in Niger State.
- **Tech Stack**: Python, Power BI, QGIS.
